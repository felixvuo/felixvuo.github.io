<img src="/images/thumb-modref.png" style="float:right; padding:1ex;" />ModRef has been an excellent
venue for presenting cutting edge research, at the work in progress stage for researchers interested
in constraint programming.  As a PhD student, I found it a valuable stepping stone, enabling me to
polish and improve my work before submitting it for publication.

This year, I am honoured to be co-chairing it with the wonderful Jordi Coll, with whom I've had the
pleasure of collaborating in the past.  The response from the community has been tremendous in
terms of forming a program committee and getting reviews done in good time.  We hope to do a little
something special to celebrate the 25th running of this workshop.
