<img src="/images/thumb-tt-trophy.png" style="float:right; padding:1ex;" />In the summer of 2025,
the maths department moved in alongside computer science into the Ian Wand building.  The table
tennis table in our courtyard stood mostly unused, as did the table-football in the communal pod.  I
organised a table sports tournament to encourage people from both departments to have some fun
getting to know each other over a game.

It was great to see so many willing participants and also the sense of community, with spectators
and a fantastic pair of trophies produced in-house in the Institute for Safe Autonomy by one our
colleagues James Hilder.  The only shame was having to present both trophies to winners from the
maths department.  Hopefully next year CS can re-gain at least one of them.
